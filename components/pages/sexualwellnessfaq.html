Sexual Wellness

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I consider when choosing the right condom for me?
Choosing the right condom depends on fit, material, and sensitivity preferences. Look for latex-free options if you have allergies.
How do lubricants enhance sexual wellness, and which type should I choose?
Lubricants reduce friction, enhance pleasure, and prevent irritation. Water-based lubricants are safe for condoms, while silicone-based last longer.
What are PrEP and PEP, and how do they help prevent HIV?
PrEP is taken daily before exposure to prevent HIV. PEP is taken after potential exposure and must be started within 72 hours.
How can I safely use sexual wellness products to prevent STIs?
Always use protection, choose medical-grade materials, and clean products thoroughly before and after use.
