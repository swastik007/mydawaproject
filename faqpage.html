Account

How do I create a MYDAWA account?
Creating a MyDawa account is simple and quick. Start by visiting the MyDawa website or app on your preferred device and clicking on the “Sign Up” or “Create Account” button. You’ll be prompted to enter your name, email address, phone number, and create a secure password. After entering these details, you’ll need to verify your information by checking your email or SMS for a verification code and entering it on the platform. Once verified, complete your profile by adding additional details like your delivery address for a seamless shopping experience. With your account set up, you can start exploring and purchasing items right away. If you face any issues during the process, our customer support team is ready to assist you.
Can I update my personal information?
Yes, you can update your personal information by visiting your account settings. This includes changing your name, phone number, and email address.
