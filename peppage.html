Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP)

PEP is an emergency medication that prevents HIV if taken within 72 hours of exposure.

PEP, taken within 72 hours of exposure, greatly reduces HIV risk. Exposure occurs through contact with infected bodily fluids like semen, vaginal fluid, or blood, often via sex or shared needles.
