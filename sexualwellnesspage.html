Protect Your Health with Safe Sex Practices

Prioritizing safe sex is essential for maintaining your sexual health and preventing STIs and unplanned pregnancies. Safe sex is not just about protection; it's about mutual respect and care for you and your partner.

  • Use condoms correctly and consistently

  • Get tested regularly for STIs

  • Discuss and respect consent with your partner

  • Take preventive measures like PrEP and PEP for HIV
