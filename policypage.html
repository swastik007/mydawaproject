When can you expect your delivery?

Orders made 8:00 am - 8:00 pm:

  • Nairobi County: Delivered within 4 hours.
  • Greater Nairobi: Delivered within 6 hours (orders after 6:00 pm by the next day, midday).
  • Rest of Kenya: Delivered within 24 hours.

Orders made after 8:00 pm:

  • Nairobi County: By 10:00 am next day.
  • Greater Nairobi: By midday next day.
  • Rest of Kenya: Within 36 hours.

You will receive an order confirmation on your mobile phone with the order number. If you do not receive the order number, then your order has not been confirmed by our system.

Seamless Pay-On-Delivery Process

  • Pay via M-Pesa after reviewing your order.
  • Ensure you load your M-Pesa wallet before delivery.

Pay-on-delivery is only available for Nairobi orders.

Discreet Shipping for Sensitive Products

All orders for sensitive items, such as sexual wellness products (e.g., contraceptives, STI/UTI medication), are shipped in plain, unmarked packaging to protect your privacy.

Delivery agents will ensure utmost discretion during handover.

Stay Updated on Your Order

  • Easily track your order in the "Your Orders" section.
